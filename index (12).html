📂 Step 1 — Upload & Preview
Upload your CSV file using the sidebar. InsightIQ will instantly read your data, detect all columns (numeric and categorical), count rows, and show you a preview. This is where your analysis journey begins!
⬅️ Upload a CSV file using the sidebar to begin!

🧹 Step 2 — Data Cleaning
Raw data is almost never perfect! This step automatically scans your dataset for duplicate rows, missing/empty values, and data quality issues. Click the buttons below to fix them — InsightIQ shows you exactly what changed and why it matters for accurate analysis.
Click buttons above to clean your data!
📈 Step 3 — Statistical Analysis
Now InsightIQ digs deep into your data! It calculates mean, median, min, max, and standard deviation for every numeric column. It also detects outliers using the IQR method, finds strong correlations between columns, and identifies trends. This is what a data scientist does — but in seconds!
Click button to run analysis!
📊 Step 4 — Auto Visualization
A picture is worth a thousand rows! InsightIQ automatically selects the best chart type for each column combination — bar charts for categories, line charts for trends, pie charts for distributions. All charts are generated instantly from your cleaned data!
📤 Step 5 — Export Results
Your analysis is complete! Now export everything — the cleaned dataset as CSV, or a full text report with all insights, statistics, trends and recommendations. Share it with your team or stakeholders with one click!
🎉 Analysis Complete! Download your results above.
